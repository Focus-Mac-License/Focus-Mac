## How to Install

1. Download the latest version of Focus for macOS.  
2. Open the DMG installer and move Focus.app to the Applications folder.  
3. Launch the app and allow it through macOS Gatekeeper if required.  

---

## Overview

<a href="#">Focus for macOS</a> is a productivity app designed to help you minimize distractions and focus on your most important tasks. It allows you to set up custom focus modes that block specific apps, websites, or notifications, giving you the space you need to work without interruptions. You can track your time, set goals, and take periodic breaks to refresh your mind and maintain high levels of productivity.

With features like website blockers, app restriction modes, and session timers, Focus empowers you to stay on task and make progress on your goals. The app helps you build habits by encouraging focused work sessions followed by regular breaks. You can track your work time and analyze how much time you’ve spent in focus mode throughout the day.

Focus integrates smoothly with macOS, ensuring that you can stay productive without needing to think about distractions. It’s the perfect tool for professionals, students, or anyone looking to boost their efficiency and reduce procrastination.
